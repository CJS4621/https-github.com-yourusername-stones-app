COLOR PALETTE
=============

Background (radial gradient, center → edge):
  #221C16  warm dark brown-black (CENTER)
  #110D0A  mid dark
  #050403  outer dark (EDGE)

Light beam (warm gold → cream):
  #C9A04C  gold base (outer beam)
  #FFD482  warm gold mid
  #FFE6A5  cream warm (light caustics, dust motes)
  #FFF5C8  bright cream-white (inner core)
  #FFFADC  brightest (sun flare at top)

Ebenezer stone:
  #8A6420  stone deep shadow
  #C9A04C  stone main body (gold — matches beam)
  #E0BC68  stone highlight (top-left chip, cross emblem)
  #6A4D18  inscription "Ebenezer"

Verse + title text:
  #C9A04C  gold (title "STONES", emphasized verse phrase, reference line)
  #FFE6A5  warm cream (main verse body)


TYPOGRAPHY
==========

Primary serif: Georgia
Acceptable substitutes: Playfair Display, Cormorant, Source Serif Pro

"STONES" title:
  - 48-56px equivalent
  - Regular weight (NOT bold)
  - Color: #C9A04C
  - Letter spacing: +3-4px

Verse body:
  - 16-18px equivalent
  - Italic
  - Color: #FFE6A5 at 85% opacity
  - Line height: 1.6-1.7

Verse reference "— 1 Samuel 7:12 (ESV)":
  - 12-13px equivalent
  - Italic
  - Color: #C9A04C at 80% opacity

The phrase "Till now the Lord has helped us" (within the verse) should be
slightly emphasized — either gold color (#C9A04C) or slightly brighter.


VERSE TEXT (exact wording — ESV)
=================================

"Then Samuel took a stone and set it up between Mizpah and Shen and called
its name Ebenezer; for he said, 'Till now the Lord has helped us.'"

— 1 Samuel 7:12 (ESV)


CANVAS
======

Target: 1080 × 1920 px (portrait)
Frame rate: 60 fps
Duration: 7.5 seconds total
Loop: NO — ends cleanly
Final frame: fade to transparent (so app home feed can show through)
